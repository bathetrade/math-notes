Problem F — Symmetries of Geometric Figures

  1. Let G be the group of symmetries of the regular hexagon. List the elements of G and show its table.

    First, the rotations about the center of the hexagon:

    R0 = 
    1 2 3 4 5 6
    1 2 3 4 5 6
    R1 = 
    1 2 3 4 5 6
    2 3 4 5 6 1
    R2 = 
    1 2 3 4 5 6
    3 4 5 6 1 2
    R3 = 
    1 2 3 4 5 6
    4 5 6 1 2 3
    R4 = 
    1 2 3 4 5 6
    5 6 1 2 3 4
    R5 = 
    1 2 3 4 5 6
    6 1 2 3 4 5

    Then, flips around the various axes:

    Fa = 
    1 2 3 4 5 6
    5 4 3 2 1 6
    Fb = 
    1 2 3 4 5 6
    6 5 4 3 2 1
    Fc = 
    1 2 3 4 5 6
    1 6 5 4 3 2
    Fd = 
    1 2 3 4 5 6
    2 1 6 5 4 3
    Fe = 
    1 2 3 4 5 6
    3 2 1 6 5 4
    Ff = 
    1 2 3 4 5 6
    4 3 2 1 6 5

    Now, the Cayley table of the group operation:

    ∘ R0 R1 R2 R3 R4 R5 Fa Fb Fc Fd Fe Ff
    R0 R0 R1 R2 R3 R4 R5 Fa Fb Fc Fd Fe Ff
    R1 R1 R2 R3 R4 R5 R0 Fb Fc Fd Fe Ff Fa
    R2 R2 R3 R4 R5 R0 R1 Fc Fd Fe Ff Fa Fb
    R3 R3 R4 R5 R0 R1 R2 Fd Fe Ff Fa Fb Fc
    R4 R4 R5 R0 R1 R2 R3 Fe Ff Fa Fb Fc Fd
    R5 R5 R0 R1 R2 R3 R4 Ff Fa Fb Fc Fd Fe
    Fa Fa Ff Fe Fd Fc Fb R0 R5 R4 R3 R2 R1
    Fb Fb Fa Ff Fe Fd Fc R1 R0 R5 R4 R3 R2
    Fc Fc Fb Fa Ff Fe Fd R2 R1 R0 R5 R4 R3
    Fd Fd Fc Fb Fa Ff Fe R3 R2 R1 R0 R5 R4
    Fe Fe Fd Fc Fb Fa Ff R4 R3 R2 R1 R0 R5
    Ff Ff Fe Fd Fc Fb Fa R5 R4 R3 R2 R1 R0

  2. List the symmetries of the rectangle, and give its table.

    First, the rotations about the center of the rectangle:

    R0 = 
    1 2 3 4
    1 2 3 4
    R2 = 
    1 2 3 4
    3 4 1 2

    Then, the flips around the two axes of symmetry:

    Fv = 
    1 2 3 4
    2 1 4 3
    Fh = 
    1 2 3 4
    4 3 2 1

    Now, the Cayley table of the group operation:

    ∘ R0 R2 Fv Fh
    R0 R0 R2 Fv Fh
    R2 R2 R0 Fh Fv
    Fv Fv Fh R0 R2
    Fh Fh Fv R2 R0

  3. Give the symmetries and tables of the letters "Z", "V", and "H".

    "Z" only has two symmetries: no movement and a half-rotation.

    R0 = 
    1 2
    1 2
    R2 = 
    1 2
    2 1

    Now, the Cayley table of the group operation:

    ∘ R0 R2
    R0 R0 R2
    R2 R2 R0

    "V" only has two symmetries: no movement and a rotation about its vertical axis. Its table will be identical to that of "Z", modulo re-labeling.

    "H" has the same symmetries as does a rectangle, so its table will be the same as that in Exercise 2 (except for re-labeling).

  4. Give the symmetries and table for a circle with three radii drawn in it at angles of 2π/3 to each other.

    R0 = 
    1 2 3
    1 2 3
    R1 = 
    1 2 3
    2 3 1
    R2 = 
    1 2 3
    3 1 2
    F1 = 
    1 2 3
    1 3 2
    F2 = 
    1 2 3
    3 2 1
    F3 = 
    1 2 3
    2 1 3

    Now, the Cayley table of the group operation:

    ∘ R0 R1 R2 F1 F2 F3
    R0 R0 R1 R2 F1 F2 F3
    R1 R1 R2 R0 F3 F1 F2
    R2 R2 R1 R0 F2 F3 F1
    F1 F1 F2 F3 R0 R1 R2
    F2 F2 F3 F1 R2 R0 R1
    F3 F3 F1 F2 R1 R2 R0
