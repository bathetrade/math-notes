Problem F — Groups Determined by Generators and Defining Equations

  1. Let G = {e, a, b, b2, ab, ab2}, satisfying the equations a2=e, b3=e, ba=ab2. Show the Cayley table of G.

    e a b b2 ab ab2
    e e a b b2 ab ab2
    a a e ab ab2 b b2
    b b ab2 b2 e a ab
    b2 b2 ab e b ab2 a
    ab ab b2 ab2 a e b
    ab2 ab2 b a ab b2 e

  2. G is called the dihedral group D4.

    Let G = {e, a, b, b2, b3, ab, ab2, ab3}, satisfying the equations a2=e, b4=e, ba=ab3. Show the Cayley table of G.

    e a b b2 b3 ab ab2 ab3
    e e a b b2 b3 ab ab2 ab3
    a a e ab ab2 ab3 b b2 b3
    b b ab3 b2 b3 e a ab ab2
    b2 b2 ab2 b3 e b ab3 a ab
    b3 b3 ab e b b2 ab2 ab3 a
    ab ab b3 ab2 ab3 a e b b2
    ab2 ab2 b2 ab3 a ab b3 e b
    ab3 ab3 b a ab ab2 b2 b3 e

  3. G is called the quaternion group.

    Let G = {e, a, b, b2, b3, ab, ab2, ab3}, satisfying the equations a4=e, a2=b2, ba=ab3. Show the Cayley table of G.

    e a b b2 b3 ab ab2 ab3
    e e a b b2 b3 ab ab2 ab3
    a a b2 ab ab2 ab3 b3 e b
    b b ab3 b2 b3 e a ab ab2
    b2 b2 ab2 b3 e b ab3 a ab
    b3 b3 ab e b b2 ab2 ab3 a
    ab ab b ab2 ab3 a b2 b3 e
    ab2 ab2 e ab3 a ab b b2 b3
    ab3 ab3 b3 a ab ab2 e b b2

  4. Let G = {e, a, b, c, ab, bc, ac, abc} be a commutative group satisfying the equation a2 = b2 = c2. Show the Cayley table of G.

    e a b c ab bc ac abc
    e e a b c ab bc ac abc
    a a e ab ac b abc c bc
    b b ab e bc a c abc ac
    c c ac bc e abc b a ab
    ab ab b a abc e ac bc c
    bc bc abc c b ac e ab a
    ac ac c abc a bc ab e b
    abc abc bc ac ab c a b e
