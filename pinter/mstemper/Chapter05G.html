Problem G — Cayley Diagrams

Write the table of the groups having the following Cayley diagrams:

  1. e a b ab
    e e a b ab
    a a e ab b
    b b ab e a
    ab ab b a e

  2. e a b ab ba aba
    e e a b ab ba aba
    a a e ab b aba ba
    b b ba e aba a ab
    ab ab aba a ba e b
    ba ba b aba e ab a
    aba aba ab ba a b e

  3. e a b ab ba aba bab abab
    e e a b ab ba aba bab abab
    a a e ab b aba ba abab bab
    b b ba e bab a abab ab aba
    ab ab aba a abab e bab b ba
    ba ba b bab e abab a aba ab
    aba aba ab abab a bab e ba b
    bab bab abab ba aba b ab e a
    abab abab bab aba ba ab b a e

  4. This is the "dihedral group", D4, from Problem F, Exercise 2.

    e a b b2 b3 ba b2a b3a
    e e a b b2 b3 ba b2a b3a
    a a e b3a b2a ba b3 b2 b
    b b ba b2 b3 e b2a b3a a
    b2 b2 b2a b3 e b b3a a ba
    b3 b3 b3a e b b2 a ba b2a
    ba ba b a b3a b2a e b3 b2
    b2a b2a b2 ba a b3a b e b3
    b3a b3a b3 b2a ba a b2 b e

  5. e a b b2 b3 ab ab2 ab3
    e e a b b2 b3 ab ab2 ab3
    a a e ab ab2 ab3 b b2 b3
    b b ab b2 b3 e ab2 ab3 a
    b2 b2 ab2 b3 e b ab3 a ab

    ab ab b ab2 ab3 a b2 b3 e
    ab2 ab2 b2 ab3 a ab b3 e b
    ab3 ab3 b3 a ab ab2 e b b2

  6. e a b b2 ab ba bab ab2 aba b2a b2ab bab2
    e e a b b2 ab ba bab ab2 aba b2a b2ab bab2
    a a e ab ab2 b aba b2a b2 ba bab bab2 b2ab
