Problem A — Computing Elements of S6

Given the following permutations:

f = 
1 2 3 4 5 6
6 1 3 5 4 2
g = 
1 2 3 4 5 6
2 3 1 6 5 4
h = 
1 2 3 4 5 6
3 1 6 4 5 2

  1. Compute the following:

    1. f-1 =

      1 2 3 4 5 6
      2 6 3 5 4 1

    2. g-1 =

      1 2 3 4 5 6
      3 1 2 6 5 4

    3. h-1 =

      1 2 3 4 5 6
      2 6 1 4 5 3

    4. f∘g =

      1 2 3 4 5 6
      1 3 6 2 4 5

    5. g∘f =

      1 2 3 4 5 6
      4 2 1 5 6 3

  2. f∘(g∘h) =

    1 2 3 4 5 6
    6 1 5 2 4 3

  3. g∘h-1 =

    1 2 3 4 5 6
    3 4 2 6 5 1

  4. h∘g-1∘f-1 =

    1 2 3 4 5 6
    3 4 1 5 2 6

  5. g∘g∘g =

    1 2 3 4 5 6
    1 2 3 6 5 4
