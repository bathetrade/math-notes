Problem D — Group Elements and Their Inverses

Let a∈G, b∈G, and C∈G, with e as the identity.

  1. Prove ab=e → ba=e.

    ab e given
    abb-1 eb-1 post-multiply by b-1
    ae eb-1 inverse
    a b-1 identity
    ba bb-1 pre-multiply by b
    ba e inverse

    Note that we could have used the fact that the inverse of the inverse of an element is the element itself.

  2. Prove that abc=e → cab=e

    abc e given
    (ab)c e associativity
    c(ab) e inverses commute
    cab e associativity

    Prove that abc=e → bca=e

    abc e given
    a(bc) e associativity
    (bc)a e inverses commute
    bca e associativity

  3. State a generalization of the previous two exercises.

    If the product of n elements of a group is the identity element, it remains so no matter in what order the terms are multiplied.

  4. Prove that xay=a-1 → yax=a-1.

    xay a-1 given
    axay aa-1 pre-multiply by a
    axay e inverse
    (axa)y e associativity
    y(axa) e inverses commute
    (yax)a e associativity
    (yax)aa-1 ea-1 post-multiply by a-1
    (yax)e ea-1 inverse
    yax a-1 identity

  5. Let a, b, and c be their own inverses.

    Prove ab=c → bc=a

    ab c given
    aab ac pre-multiply by a
    eb ac a is its own inverse
    b ac identity
    bc acc post-multiply by c
    bc ae c is its own inverse
    bc a identity

    Prove ab=c → ca=b

    ab c given
    cab cc pre-multiply by c
    cab e c is its own inverse
    cabb eb post-multiply by b
    cae eb b is its own inverse
    ca b identity

  6. Prove that if abc is its own inverse, bca is its own inverse.

    (abc)(abc) e given
    abcabc e associativity
    a-1abcabc a-1e pre-multiply by a-1
    ebcabc a-1e inverse
    bcabc a-1 identity
    bcabcc-1 a-1c-1 post-multiply by c-1
    bcabe a-1c-1 inverse
    bcab a-1c-1 identity
    bcabb-1 a-1c-1b-1 post-multiply by b-1
    bcae a-1c-1b-1 inverse
    bca a-1c-1b-1 identity
    bca (bca)-1 product of inverses is inverse of reverse product

    Prove that if abc is its own inverse, cab is its own inverse.

    (abc)(abc) e given
    abcabc e associativity
    abcabcc-1 ec-1 post-multiply by c-1
    abcabe ec-1 inverse
    abcab c-1 identity
    a-1abcab a-1c-1 pre-multiply by a-1
    ebcab a-1c-1 inverse
    bcab a-1c-1 identity
    b-1bcab b-1a-1c-1 pre-multiply by b-1
    ecab b-1a-1c-1 inverse
    cab b-1a-1c-1 identity
    cab (cab)-1 product of inverses is inverse of reverse product

  7. Prove that if a and b are their own inverses, ba = (ab)-1.

    ba b-1a b = b-1
    b-1a-1 a = a-1
    (ab)-1 product of inverses is inverse of reverse product

  8. Prove that a=a-1 iff aa=e.

    If:

    a a-1 given
    aa aa-1 pre-multiply by a
    aa e inverse

    Only if:

    aa e given
    aaa-1 ea-1 post-multiply by a-1
    ae ea-1 inverse
    a a-1 identity

  9. Let c=c-1. Prove ab=c iff abc=e.

    If:

    ab c given
    abc cc post-multiply by c
    abc e c is its own inverse

    Only if:

    abc e given
    abcc ec post-multiply by c
    abe ec c is its own inverse
    ab c identity
