Problem A — Solving Equations in Groups

Solve each of the following equations for x in terms of a, b, c.

  1. axb = c

    axb c
    axbb-1 cb-1 post-multiply by b-1
    axe cb-1 inverse
    ax cb-1 identity
    a-1ax a-1cb-1 pre-multiply by a-1
    ex a-1cb-1 inverse
    x a-1cb-1 identity

  2. x2b = xa-1c

    x2b xa-1c
    x2bb-1 xa-1cb-1 post-multiply by b-1
    x2e xa-1cb-1 inverse
    x2 xa-1cb-1 identity
    x-1x2 x-1xa-1cb-1 pre-multiply by x-1
    x-1xx x-1xa-1cb-1 definition of x2
    ex ea-1cb-1 inverse
    x a-1cb-1 identity

  3. x2a = bxc-1 and acx = xac

    x2a bxc-1
    x2ac bxc-1c post-multiply by c
    x2ac bxe inverse
    x2ac bx identity
    xxac bx definition of x2
    xacx bx acx = xac
    xacxx-1 bxx-1 post-multiply by x-1
    xace be inverse
    xac b identity
    xacc-1 bc-1 post-multiply by c-1
    xae bc-1 inverse
    xa bc-1 identity
    xaa-1 bc-1a-1 post-multiply by a-1
    xe bc-1a-1 inverse
    x bc-1a-1 identity

  4. ax2 = b and x3 = e

    ax2 b
    a-1ax2 a-1b pre-multiply by a-1
    ex2 a-1b inverse
    x2 a-1b identity
    xx2 xa-1b pre-multiply by x
    x3 xa-1b definition of x3
    e xa-1b x3 = e
    eb-1 xa-1bb-1 post-multiply by b-1
    eb-1 xa-1e inverse
    b-1 xa-1 identity
    b-1a xa-1a post-multiply by a
    b-1a xe inverse
    b-1a x identity

  5. x2 = a2 and x5 = e

    x5 e
    xxxxx e definition of x5
    x2x2x e definition of x2
    a2a2x e x2 = a2
    a2a2xx-1 ex-1 post-multiply by x-1
    a2a2xx-1 x-1 identity
    a2a2e x-1 inverse
    a2a2 x-1 identity
    aaaa x-1 definition of a2
    a4 x-1 definition of a4
    a-4 x property of inverses

  6. (xax)3 = bx and x2a = (xa)-1

    (xax)3 bx
    xaxxaxxax bx definition of (xax)3
    xax2ax2ax bx definition of (xax)2
    xa(xa)-1(xa)-1x bx x2a = (xa)-1
    xaa-1x-1a-1x-1x bx (xa)-1 = a-1x-1
    xex-1a-1e bx inverse
    xx-1a-1 bx identity
    ea-1 bx inverse
    a-1 bx identity
    b-1a-1 b-1bx premultiply by b-1
    b-1a-1 ex inverse
    b-1a-1 x identity
    (ab)-1 x (ab)-1 = b-1a-1
