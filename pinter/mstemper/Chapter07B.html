Problem B — Examples of Groups of Permutations

  1. Let G be the subset of S4 consisting of the following permutations:

    e = 
    1 2 3 4
    1 2 3 4
    f = 
    1 2 3 4
    2 1 4 3
    g = 
    1 2 3 4
    3 4 1 2
    h = 
    1 2 3 4
    4 3 2 1

    Show that G is a group of permutations and write its table.

    Composition of functions is associative. The permutation "e" is the identity element. Each of f, g, and h is its own inverse.

    ∘ e f g h
    e e f g h
    f f e h g
    g g h e f
    h h g f e

  2. List the elements of the cyclic subgroup of S6 generated by:

    f = 
    1 2 3 4 5 6
    2 3 4 1 6 5

    The elements (other than f) generated by f are:

    f2 = 
    1 2 3 4 5 6
    3 4 1 2 5 6
    f3 = 
    1 2 3 4 5 6
    4 1 2 3 6 5
    f4 = e = 
    1 2 3 4 5 6
    1 2 3 4 5 6

  3. Find a four-element Abelian subgroup of S5 and write its table.

    Such a group is generated by:

    f = 
    1 2 3 4 5
    2 3 4 1 5

    Its table is:

    ∘ e f f2 f3
    e e f f2 f3
    f f f2 f3 e
    f2 f2 f3 e f
    f3 f3 e f f2

  4. The subgroup of S5 generated by:

    f = 
    1 2 3 4 5
    2 1 3 4 5
    g = 
    1 2 3 4 5
    1 2 4 5 3

    has six elements. List them, and write the table of this group.

    h = g2 = 
    1 2 3 4 5
    1 2 5 3 4
    k = fg = 
    1 2 3 4 5
    2 1 4 5 3
    l = fg2 = 
    1 2 3 4 5
    2 1 5 3 4

    This group's table is:

    ∘ e f g h k l
    e e f g h k l
    f f e k l g h
    g g k h e l f
    h h l e g f k
    k k g l f h e
    l l h f k e g
